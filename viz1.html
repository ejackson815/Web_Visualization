Max Temperature

As expected, there is a direct correlation between temperature and distance from equator. The further we move away from the equator the colder the weather becomes and vice versa. So if you need a break from the frigid weather, we highly recommend you vacation to a destination close to the equator. Perhaps New Fiji?
