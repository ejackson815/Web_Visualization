Humidity

What does this graph represent? First lets define humidity: Atmospheric pressure or the quantity that represents the amount of water vapor in the atmoshphere. In the graph above we see that the higher concentration of humidity volumes lies between the tropic of cancer (23.5 degree North of equator) and tropic of Capricorn (23.5 degrees south of equator). In this range we see the larger percentage of precipitation thus resulting in higher humidity volumes as water vapor is more abundant.
